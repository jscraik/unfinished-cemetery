## Scope

This page covers security issues in this repository.

## Report a Security Issue

Do not open a public issue for vulnerabilities.

Use private reporting:

- <https://github.com/jscraik/unfinished-cemetery/security/advisories/new>

If you cannot use that form, contact the maintainer:

- <https://github.com/jscraik>

## Include This in Your Report

- What the issue is.
- Why it is risky.
- Steps to reproduce.
- Files, scripts, or commands involved.

## Response Targets

- First reply: within 3 business days.
- Triage update: within 7 business days.

## Disclosure

Please keep reports private until a fix is ready.
